1. HOW TO FILE AND COMPLETE THIS APPLICATION FORM


  1. This form must be accompanied with the appropriate application fee and can only be filed here on our virtual private network.
  2. All information must be saved in pdf format and downloaded in each section
  3. Please ensure that all information required on the form is completed.
  4. Where tick boxes are provided, please ensure you tick all those boxes that apply.
  5. If there is insufficient room on the form to provide all the required information, you can add extra pages as needed
  6. Additional information - in addition to completing this form, if the application requires you to provide further information you must include all documents, information or evidence you wish Te Kooti Rangatira Ateha to consider.
